Econ

Join users around the world with an upcoming simple, fun, and realistic economic Discord bot.


What is all about?

Economy

Econ has an evolving economy, with many methods of earning money by begging, working, gambling, supply & demand, auctions, and much more.


Competive Community

Econ provides a thriving and insistent economy, with everyone's eyes on being the richest. Crimes, Heists, Robberys can all be used to attempt to steal as much money as you can from users, even with your friends!


Monthly Updates

Econ is currently in Alpha; with its has command-rich updates monthly, each update more and more expanding the bot's diversity against other discord bots.
